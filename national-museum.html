About the wing

The regional picture

As one of the National Museum of the Philippines' regional branches, this Davao outpost widens the lens beyond the city itself — its collection spans archaeological finds, ethnographic material from Mindanao's indigenous peoples, and specimens from the island's natural history.

It pairs well with Museo Dabawenyo: where that museum tells Davao's own story, this one places it inside the broader Mindanao context.

Good to know before you go
  • Free admission, as with all National Museum branches nationwide.
  • Closed Mondays and on national holidays — check ahead around long weekends.
  • Bag checks are standard at the entrance.
  • Some galleries rotate temporary exhibits — check the events section for what's currently on.
